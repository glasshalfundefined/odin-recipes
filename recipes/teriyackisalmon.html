Teriyacki Salmon

Description

Teriyacki Salmon has a delicious sweet and savory soy flavor, follow this recipe for a flaky, juicy salmon dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 (6 ounce) salmon steaks
